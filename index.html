Privacy Policy

Steady Wellness App  ·  Last updated: June 5, 2025

Overview

Steady is a private wellness app built for personal journaling, mood tracking, and goal setting. Your privacy is fundamental to how this app works — everything stays on your device.

Data We Collect

Steady does not collect any data. All information you enter — including journal entries, mood logs, goals, and app settings — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage.

  • No accounts or sign-ups required
  • No data is transmitted to any server
  • No analytics or tracking of any kind
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data

App Lock & PIN

If you choose to enable the app lock, your PIN is stored as a salted hash on your device only. It is never transmitted or accessible outside of your device.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transmit your data to anyone — because we never have access to it in the first place.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time from the Settings screen inside Steady. Uninstalling the app will also permanently remove all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

Steady does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Because no data leaves your device, the app poses no data privacy risk to users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date at the top.
